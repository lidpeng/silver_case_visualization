      5. Conclusion

      How long can silver prices continue to rise? The answer may not depend on silver itself, but on what kind of era we are in.

      Short-term, due to excessive prior gains, abundant profit-taking, and macroeconomic uncertainty, silver faces risks of sharp correction and volatility.

      Medium-term (1-3 years), as long as global inventory cannot be substantially replenished and substitute technologies have not been widely adopted, the core contradiction of the supply-demand gap cannot be resolved. This determines that silver's bottom will continue to rise.
